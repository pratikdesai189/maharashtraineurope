Why Use Sahayak for Your Europe-India Travel?

Navigating international flights between Europe and Maharashtra (India) can be stressful, especially for elderly parents or when sending urgent documents. Sahayak connects the trusted Marathi diaspora to provide safe, community-driven travel assistance.

Elderly Flight Companions

Find a Marathi-speaking travel buddy to help your parents navigate layovers, immigration, and language barriers across European airports.

Community Courier

Need to send urgent letters, medicines, or parcels? Connect with verified community members traveling between India and Europe.

Trusted & Safe

Built by and for the Marathi diaspora. While we advise personal verification, the platform relies on strong community bonds and mutual respect.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a travel companion for my parents flying to Europe?
Using Sahayak, you can submit your parents' flight details or browse our active listings to find other Indian community members traveling on the same route. Connect via WhatsApp to coordinate travel assistance at the airport or during layovers.
Can I find someone to carry urgent documents to India?
Yes! Many community members offer carrier services. Look for listings tagged with 'Letter / Documents' and contact the traveler directly. Always ensure safety and mutual trust before exchanging documents.
Is it safe to use community travel assistance?
Sahayak connects members of the Marathi and Indian diaspora in Europe. While it is built on community trust, we advise all users to verify identities and use common sense before handing over documents, money, or goods.
